A new way to use empathy.


My journey to design started in Eindhoven, where I studied Design Technology as a higher level concentration in my International Baccalaureate diploma. While the course was highly industrial design focused, a key chapter caught my eye.

User Centered Design.

The notion that I could use empathy in combination with a little old fashioned problem solvin’ sounded amazing to me, and thus my design career started.

Years and many design tools later, I find myself still feeling the same excitement as when I first put together my first mobile screen.

I do not know what the future holds, but I do know there are so many more exciting opportunities, and adventures on the way.
