A new way to use empathy.


My journey to design started in Eindhoven, where I studied Design Technology as a higher level concentration in my International Baccalaureate diploma. While the course was highly industrial design focused, a key chapter caught my eye.

User Centered Design.

The notion that I could use empathy in combination with a little old fashioned problem solvin’ sounded amazing to me, and thus my design career started.

Years and many design tools later, I find myself still feeling the same excitement as when I first put together my first mobile screen on the 30 day free trial for Sketch. I often look back at that project just to remind myself how far I have come.

Now, I find myself within a new journey. New roads to explore, and new problems to solve. I am super excited about what is going to unfold in the next couple of years, and I can't wait to see where it takes me.
