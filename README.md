# Ishaan Bose — Design Portfolio

Personal design portfolio of Ishaan Bose, live at [ishaanbose.com](https://ishaanbose.com). 

## About

Product designer with a focus on developer tools and complex systems, currently at HashiCorp working on HCP Terraform — the industry-standard platform for infrastructure as code. Promoted from Associate to Product Designer in 13 months, and now the sole designer on the Terraform Core-Cloud team serving 2,000+ customers worldwide.

Previously: UX Designer at Demandbase (B2B marketing platform) and UX/UI Design Intern at Institutional Shareholder Services. UCSD alum, where design was explored through the lens of Cognitive Science. Originally discovered design through the IB Design Technology program while studying in the Netherlands.
