HCP Packer
Image Insights

Context

9 week long intern project on HCP Packer. HCP Packer is HashiCorp’s tool to build and provision machine images at scale. Machine images are used in DevOps and IT to segment their end-user groups by function due to the software/OS differences.

Role

Timeline: Summer 2022

Role: Lead Designer on HCP Packer

Skills: UX Design, UX Research, Interaction design, Data visualization

Project

Goal: Scope out and deliver image usage insights on HCP Packer for users who own infrastructure in their organization, or roadmap advocates, to help them monitor and understand image health.

Problem

Practioners need image usage data to inform their machine image lifecycle management at scale. Currently, Packer does not have an easy way to extract this data. We want to provide information contextually to inform the practitioner and aid them in their decision making process.

UX Research

Completed a 2 week research sprint that was primarily conducted through an external survey that was distributed through Reddit

To further understand user needs, we need to decompose the problem into a How might we that we can solve.
