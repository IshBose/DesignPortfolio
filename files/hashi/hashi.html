Overview

Project -


Scope out and deliver image usage insights on HCP Packer

Why this was added to the backlog -


Next step in HashiCorp product development process. Ship, then add insights.

Who -


Users who own infrastructure in their organization, or roadmap advocates

3

Problem

Practioners need image usage data to inform their machine image lifecycle management at scale. Currently, Packer does not have an easy way to extract this data. We want to provide information contextually to inform the practitioner and aid them in their decision making process.

Key Takeaways


Some of my core takeaways from this experience, and working at HashiCorp -

One thing I would do differently if I had more time or in another situation would be to try to relate this to greater company OKR's. This way I could make sure that what I am designing is bringing value to the business at the forefront, and develops my product design skills in learning how to prove value to my work. Unfortunately due to limited resources, I was not able to meet as often as I would have liked to with product, which led to quite a bit of the product deliverables being defined by myself, which was challenging, but brought forward a lot of growth.
